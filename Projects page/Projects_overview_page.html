Design & Creativity

Design and Creativity course was focused on creating a portfolio that is a collection of sketches. Some of the tasks were to draw a representation of ourselves, while others were focused on storyboards for design ideas.

Research for Design II

Research for Design (part II) is specifically oriented on Ethnographic research. Ethnography in design involves a lot of naturalistic observation, interviewing and exploring. It also studies behavioural patterns and based on those it inspires design.

Skills Lab

In the Skills Lab course we have been working on creating the visuals of a website related to the Hague. The topic was a free choice and I have decided to design a website that is meant for tourists. It contains sections as advise where to go, what is worth trying and where to meet locals.

Introduction to UX

In the introduction to UX course we have been learning what are the user experience factors of any product.

There are several cathegories that influence the exprience of user: Usability, Engagement & Meaning. One of the final works was a poster with evaluation of an app of our choice based on criteria learned throughout the course.
